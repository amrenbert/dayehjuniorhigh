# Unit 1: Reading & Speaking Activity
**Date:** June 2026  
**Instructions:** Read the short passage below, then answer the questions with a partner.

---

## Part 1: The Reading Passage
"The early morning is the quietest part of the day. For many people, it is the perfect time to clear their mind, enjoy a hot beverage, or focus on a personal hobby before the busy workday begins."

---

## Part 2: Discussion Questions
1. What do you like to do to decompress before your workday starts?
2. Do you consider yourself an early riser or a night owl? Explain why.
3. Vocabulary check: What do you think the word **decompress** means based on the text?
